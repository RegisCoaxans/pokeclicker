Play Voltorb Flip!

Defeat as many Voltorb as possible within 5 minutes using your Flip Attack only.
You will be presented with a board. Increase your Flip Attack by picking value cards.
If you flip a Voltorb, though, the attack bonus from the current level is lost.
Find all ×2 and ×3 to continue to the next level.
